## Displacement-Time Graphs
The gradient of a displacement-time graph at a particular time gives us the velocity of the object at that time.
$$
\large \text
{Gradient (Velocity)} =
\frac
	{\text {Change in Displacement}}
	{\text {Change in Time}}
$$
